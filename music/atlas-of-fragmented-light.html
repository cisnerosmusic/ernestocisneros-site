The blue breath of the immutable

Index · Regions

In a world that never was, where maps do not delimit but dissolve, there exists a land charted by the oscillation between certainty and mystery. Its coasts are made of questions, and its mountains of trembling memories. Every river flows toward a sea called Mare Incognitum, where knowledge fragments into light and the horizon recedes like a promise that is never reached. This atlas does not guide; it invites you to become lost. Each musical theme is a region: a translucent dance, a breath, a memory that bleeds light.
