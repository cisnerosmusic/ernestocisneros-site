International Work

Since 1999, Ernesto has performed and worked internationally across England, Spain, Italy, Germany, France, Russia, South Korea, Argentina, Venezuela, Panama, Costa Rica, Nicaragua, Mexico, the United States, and Canada, collaborating with various artists and developing pedagogical projects alongside his compositional work.
