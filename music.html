Music

Music is where everything begins.

I have been studying music since I was six years old, and almost forty years later, I continue to do so. It is not a discipline I practice; it is a logic I have installed as a creator and composer. Every other form of expression I explore is, in some way, an extension of this first encounter with art.

Music taught me to listen before speaking, to structure emotion, and to find beauty in the tension between order and chaos. It remains my primary language.
