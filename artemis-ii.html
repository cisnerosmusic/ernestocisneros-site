Commander
Reid Wiseman
NASA · USN Captain

Baltimore native. Naval aviator and test pilot with over 2,000 flight hours in F/A-18 combat aircraft. Selected by NASA in 2009 from 3,500 applicants. ISS veteran with 165 days in space and two spacewalks. Former Chief of the Astronaut Office (2020-2022). At 50, he's the oldest person to travel beyond low Earth orbit. After losing his wife Carroll to cancer in 2020, he considers single parenting his greatest personal achievement.

Pilot
Victor Glover
NASA · USN Captain
First Black Astronaut to the Moon

Born in Pomona, California. Cal Poly graduate with four academic degrees including engineering, systems, and military science. Naval combat pilot with 3,000+ flight hours in 40+ aircraft and 400+ carrier landings. Completed 24 combat missions. Piloted the first operational SpaceX Crew Dragon to ISS in 2020, spending 168 days in orbit with 4 spacewalks. His callsign is "Ike" (I Know Everything).

Mission Specialist
Christina Koch
NASA · Electrical Engineer
First Woman to the Moon

Born in Grand Rapids, Michigan and raised in Jacksonville, North Carolina. Holds dual bachelor's degrees (electrical engineering and physics) and a master's from NC State. Worked on space instrumentation at NASA Goddard and spent a year in Antarctica at the South Pole station as part of firefighting and rescue teams. Completed 328 consecutive days on ISS—the female record—and performed the first three all-female spacewalks with Jessica Meir.

Mission Specialist
Jeremy Hansen
CSA · RCAF Colonel
First Canadian & Non-American

Born in London, Ontario and raised on a farm near Ailsa Craig. Joined Air Cadets at age 12 and earned his pilot's license at 17. CF-18 combat pilot with the Royal Canadian Air Force and NORAD experience. Holds a BSc in Space Science and a master's in Physics from Royal Military College. Selected by CSA in 2009, he was the first non-American to lead a NASA astronaut class. His mission patch features seven sacred animals from Anishinaabe Indigenous teachings.
