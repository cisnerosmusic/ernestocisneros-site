Tezos Collection

Collected Works

This section brings together a selection of works I have collected on Tezos, an ecosystem where experimentation, accessibility, and community have played a defining role in the evolution of digital art.

My journey on Tezos is closely tied to a moment of collective effervescence, where artists from diverse backgrounds converged to explore new forms of expression. In that context, collecting became an act of connection. Each piece here reflects a dialogue with creators who embraced openness, resilience, and innovation.

Unlike more established markets, Tezos cultivated a space where value was not dictated solely by scale or speculation, but by presence, consistency, and creative authenticity. Many of the works in this collection emerged from that spirit, raw, immediate, and deeply human.

These pieces are not just artifacts of a network. They are traces of a movement, one that redefined how art could be shared, supported, and remembered.

This is not just a collection. It is a memory of a living community.
